## FAQ

**Q: What if I don't have land?**
A: Your skills and time are valuable. That's where most people start.

**Q: What if my offerings don't sell?**
A: Adjust pricing, improve descriptions, or look for what's needed in your bioregion and build toward that.

**Q: What about liability?**
A: Agreements include terms. Disputes are rare when everything is transparent and traceable to pNFTs.

**Q: Can I list things for free?**
A: Yes. Community service earns care credits, which factor into your UBI.

**Q: What if I'm not good at describing things?**
A: The LLM helps you. Just talk naturally.

**Q: What about seasonal offerings?**
A: List with availability windows. Pause and unpause as needed.

**Q: Can I list for someone else?**
A: You can help them, but offerings must come from their pNFT. No intermediaries extracting value.
